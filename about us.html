OUR HISTORY


"OUR PURPOSE IN THIS LIFE
IS TO HELP OTHERS, AND IF YOU
CANT'T HELP THEM, ATLEAST DON'T HURT THEM"


Wang Charity is a non-governmental organization that was founded in 2003 by Wang Family with present CEO Wang Drukpa. The organization's main mission is to support and care for orphans in Bhutan, particularly those affected by starvation and homeless. Wang Orphan Age Charity provides a variety of services to orphans, including educational support, medical care, and emotional counseling. The organization also works to raise awareness about the issues facing orphans in Bhutan and advocates for better policies to support them. Over the years, Charity has received support from various sources, including corporations, foundations, and individual donors. The organization has also received recognition for its work, including being awarded the Bhutan Best service Award in 2020. Overall, Wang Orphan Age Charity has made a significant impact in improving the lives of orphans in Bhutan.
